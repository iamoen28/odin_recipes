Sinigang

Description

Wan't to taste this asim kilig experience? Just follow these few steps.

Ingredients

  • Onions (Sibuyas)
  • Tomatoes (Kamatis)
  • Tamarind powder mix (Optional)
  • Pork (Baboy)
  • Salt (Asin)
  • Fish sauce (Patis)
  • String beans (Sitaw)
  • Water spinach (Kangkong)
  • Taro (Gabi)
  • Water (Tubig)

Steps

  1. Cut the pork belly into large cubes approximately 1 1/2 inches across. Rinse the pork, then place into a pot and 4 cups of water. Bring the water to a moderate boil, and cook pork for 10 minutes to remove scum.
  2. Add onions and tomatoes. Pour 1 sachet of tamarind powder mix. Add some salt and fish sauce to taste.
  3. Add the water spinach and string beans and let it boil for 3 more minutes. And viola! We're done.
